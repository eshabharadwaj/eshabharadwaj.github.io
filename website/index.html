About Sleep

Sleep is a very important part of a human's daily life. It gives us energy and it plays a huge part of our physical health. One of the vital roles of sleep is to help us solidify and consolidate memories. Healthy sleep is critical for everyone, since we all need to retain information and learn skills to thrive in life.

We feel more alert, more energetic, happier, and better able to function following a good night of sleep.
